1. Easy Rafting Trip: Soca River, Slovenia

Location: Soca River, Triglav National Park, Slovenia

Duration: Half-day (approximately 3-4 hours)

Difficulty Level: Beginner

Description:

  • The Soca River, known as the Emerald River, offers an ideal rafting experience for beginners and families. The water is incredibly clear, and the alpine scenery is breathtaking.
  • The trip includes safety instructions and basic rafting techniques, followed by a calm yet exciting journey through class I to II rapids.
  • It’s the perfect opportunity to enjoy the landscape, swim in natural pools, and have a picnic on the riverbanks.

2. Moderate Rafting Trip: Colorado River, Grand Canyon, USA

Location: Colorado River, Grand Canyon, Arizona, USA

Duration: 3 days and 2 nights

Difficulty Level: Intermediate

Description:

  • This trip offers an unforgettable experience through one of the world's most famous natural wonders. The Colorado River rapids range from class II to IV, providing a good mix of thrills and challenges.
  • In addition to rafting, participants will have the opportunity to hike the Grand Canyon trails and camp under the stars at the bottom of the canyon.
  • The trip includes all meals, camping gear, and experienced guides who share stories and information about the geology and history of the area.

3. Extreme Rafting Trip: Zambezi River, Zambia/Zimbabwe

Location: Zambezi River, below Victoria Falls, Zambia/Zimbabwe

Duration: 5 days and 4 nights

Difficulty Level: Advanced

Description:

  • The Zambezi River, with its class V rapids, is one of the most challenging and thrilling rafting destinations in the world. This trip is designed for experienced rafters seeking a true adventure.
  • In addition to intense rapids, participants can enjoy spectacular landscapes and wildlife sightings along the river.
  • The trip includes professional guides, all necessary equipment, gourmet meals along the river, and camping in isolated and beautiful locations.
