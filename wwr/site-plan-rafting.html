Overview

Purpose

Rafting is an exhilarating outdoor sport that involves navigating a river or other bodies of water using an inflatable raft. Typically done in whitewater or rough water, rafting requires teamwork, physical exertion, and skillful maneuvering to navigate through rapids, waves, and obstacles.

Audience

Come enjoy with us the rafting adventure! Where you will have a exceptional experience navigating in a river and feel the real adrenalin, irrespective of your age! In rafting, participants use paddles to steer and propel the raft downstream while working together to navigate the challenges of the river
